Capabilities

  • Installed Capacity: With an installed capacity of 5000 MT forgings per annum, we have the capability to meet diverse customer demands efficiently and effectively.
  • Range: Our forging capabilities span from 1 kg to 20kg for Carbon and Alloy Steel, allowing us to cater to a wide range of applications and industries.
  • Focus: At the core of our operations lies a relentless focus on technology, functionality, and uncompromising quality. We strive to deliver products that exceed customer expectations in terms of performance and durability.
  • CRM Keynote: Our strategic Customer Relationship Management (CRM) approach ensures that we build and maintain strong partnerships with our clients. By understanding their unique needs and requirements, we can provide tailored solutions and exceptional service.
  • Customization: We are committed to leveraging our resources and expertise to deliver customised solutions that address the specific challenges and preferences of our customers. Whether it's modifying existing products or developing entirely new solutions, we are dedicated to meeting customer needs.
  • Dies Crafted: Our state-of-the-art VMC Machines enable us to craft precision dies with the highest level of accuracy and consistency. This ensures that our forged products meet the strictest quality standards and specifications, resulting in superior performance and reliability.

Facilities

Our facilities are equipped with advanced machinery and equipment to ensure efficient and high-quality manufacturing processes:

  • Two Drop Forged Hammers: With capacities of 1.5 Ton and 2.5 Ton, our drop forged hammers enable precise shaping and forming of materials, resulting in superior quality forgings.
  • VMC Machine: Our Vertical Machining Center (VMC) machine is dedicated to die making, allowing us to create intricate and precise dies with accuracy and consistency.
  • CSM Machines: Our Cutting and Shearing Machines (CSM) are utilised for cutting purposes, enabling us to achieve clean and accurate cuts on various materials.
  • Induction Heating: We have induction heating equipment with capacities of 300 KWS and 450 KWS, providing controlled and efficient heating for the forging process, ensuring uniformity and consistency in material properties.
  • Short Blast Machine: Our short blast machine is used for surface preparation and finishing, enhancing the quality and appearance of our products.
